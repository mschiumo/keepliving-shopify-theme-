# KeepLiving Product Images Guide

## Mock Images Needed

Since you don't have product photos yet, here are placeholder options:

### Option 1: Placeholder Services (Recommended)
Use these services for quick mockups:

**Placeit.net** (Paid, but best)
- Upload your designs to mug/shirt templates
- Realistic mockups instantly
- ~$15/month or pay per mockup

**Printful Mockup Generator** (Free)
- https://www.printful.com/mockup-generator
- Upload designs, generate mockups
- Free to use, pay only if you order

**Placehold.co** (For development only)
- Quick placeholder images
- Not for production

### Option 2: Create Your Own
1. Design in Canva (canva.com)
2. Use smart mockups for templates
3. Download as PNG/JPG

### Where to Upload in Shopify

1. Shopify Admin → Content → Files
2. Upload all product images
3. Copy the URLs
4. Update products.csv with actual URLs

### Alternative: Use Placeholders Now
